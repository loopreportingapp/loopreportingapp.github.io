Graduate Development Program

Join the Leading Bank in the South Pacific

Bank of South Pacific (BSP) is one of the foremost and best recognised brands in Papua New Guinea (PNG) and the South Pacific. BSP is proud of the position of trust established as the market leader in the financial sector in the region and of the integral role we play in supporting our large and diverse customer base. BSP continues to look for opportunities to expand and improve our products and services.

 

BSP Graduate Development Program 2016

Developing our young people across the Pacific to be the next generation of leaders in our organisation is part of our vision to be the Leading Bank in the South Pacific Region. The BSP Graduate Development Program provides this vision for aspiring graduates to attain career development opportunities.

Applications are now open for the BSP Graduate Development Program 2016.

 

What documents do I need?
  • Your cover letter
  • Your current CV
  • Your latest academic transcript
  • Two written references with contactable referees

 

How do I qualify?
  • Must be a Papua New Guinean Citizen
  • Currently in final year of study at undergraduate level at either a local or overseas university.

 

 You must be studying in the following disciplines

  • Banking & Finance
  • Sales & Marketing
  • Accounting
  • Business Management
  • Economics
  • Information Technology
  • Human Resources
  • Law

How do I Apply

The BSP Graduate Development Program is your chance to grow with us. Apply now to take advantage of this golden opportunity.

 

Option 1) Send application on email

Complete an application form by filling the required details and attach your cover letter, updated CV, copies of your latest academic transcripts and no more than two references. Email them as attached files to jobs@bsp.com.pg

 

Option 2) Drop your applications at your nearest BSP Branch

Complete an application form by filling the required details and attach your cover letter, updated CV, copies of your latest academic transcripts and no more than two references. Drop them at your nearest BSP Branch.

 

Option 3) Drop your applications at BSP HR (if in NCD)

Complete an application form by filling the required details and attach your cover letter, updated CV, copies of your latest academic transcripts and hand deliver them at our HR Department located at BSP Boroko Banking Centre Level 2, Port Moresby, NCD.

 

When to apply

Applications Close:             Friday June 12th, 2015

 

Recruitment Process

Step 1: Apply

Applications can be accepted through email and hand delivered to nearest Branch or can be dropped at our HR Office located at Level 2 BSP Boroko Banking Centre.

 

Step 2: Application Screening

Our HR Recruitment team will screen your applications to verify your eligibility. At this stage, your relevant undergraduate degree programs, academic results, reference checks and your involvement in extra circular activities such as leadership, community based work and etc will be looked at.

 

Step 3: First Round Interviews

Candidates from the first screening will be selected for this stage. This first round of interview will be conducted onsite at the University campus and major centres within the country. This will be behavioural based in which you will be required to draw examples of your experiences as an indication for us to predict your capability to handling job requirements.

 

Step 4: Psychometric Assessment

Shortlisted candidates selected from step 3 will then undergo an online psychometric assessment which is of international standards used by many reputable corporate companies. You will be assessed on areas in;

  1. Personality
  2. Critical Reasoning
  3. Problem Solving
  4. Leadership

The results of this test help us to identify strengths and weaknesses and how best BSP can develop you if you are successful.

 

Step 5: Final Selection Invitation for Interview  

Shortlisted candidates from step 4 are then selected by respective Strategic Business Unit General Managers. This provides an opportunity for the General Managers in BSP to learn more about you. After the interview you will be given a tour within the Strategic Business Unit work area to show how we work and the area you will work for if successful.

 

Step 6: Job Offer

If you are successful, you will be contacted by one of our HR Recruitment Coordinators for a BSP employment offer. You will be required to reply back on your decision of accepting or rescinding the offer within 14 days of receiving the offer letter from us.

Your employment status is subject to successful passing of your pre-employment medical check. Our HR will organise for your pre-employment medical check with all medical expenses paid for by BSP.

 

Step 7: Induction / Job Placement

As part of our on boarding you will join other new graduates through a four days induction program. You will then be placed at your work location after the completion of the induction program.

 

Step 8: Career Pathway  

After the completion of step 7, HR and the respective Business Unit Heads will complete a 3 year career pathway plan, including development programs to facilitate your personal development and advancement in the Bank. HR will coordinate this plan covering your personal values, strengths, areas that require improvement and your career choice in BSP.
